About Us

At Sweet Tooth, we believe in the power of baked goods to bring people together. Here's a little about our story.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Sweet Tooth started with a simple mission: to bring fresh, delicious baked goods to our community. Our founder and lead baker, Cynthia Okbandrias, bakes all types of customomised sweet goods from her home kitchen. Just contact us and she'll be baking your goods in no time. Her sweet delights are made with love, passion, and dedication, using the finest ingredients to create treats that make every day a little sweeter.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use the best ingredients to ensure every bite is perfect.
  • Community: We are passionate about supporting our local community.
  • Passion: Every product is made with love and dedication.
  • Freshness: Our baked treats are made fresh daily.
  • Customisabilty: We fulfill your custom requests to the T.
